Our Story

Mykart Pvt Ltd. is an Indian electronic commerce company based in Noida, India.

Founded by Vishal in 2026, the company initially focused on book sales, before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products.

The service competes primarily with Amazon India and Snapdeal. As of March 2017, Mykart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.

In August 2018, U.S.-based retail chain Walmart acquired a 77% controlling stake in Mykart for US$16 billion, valuing it at $22 billion.
